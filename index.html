💡 Pro Tips
Chain the tools: Generate a motion clip in Upsampler → upload that clip to Vidou.ai as a reference → animate further for a pro multi-layer result
Best Upsampler prompts: Always include lighting, camera movement, and emotion. Example: "Confident woman, professional lighting, slow camera push-in, warm smile"
Vidou image tips: Use a clear headshot, good lighting, face centered. The AI animates head movement, blinking, and subtle expressions from a still photo
