Step 3 — Shotstack: Assembles the video — voice + dark cinematic background + animated captions + AffiliateMediaHub intro/outro.
Step 4 — YouTube API: Uploads the finished MP4 with Becky's title, description, and tags. Sets to Public. Returns the live link.
